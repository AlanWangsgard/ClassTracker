What is Class-Ifides?

Class-Ifides is intended to help students by providing a platform to rate the classes from your school. If we do not already have your course, or school, you can create an account and add them for others to see as well. We want to make everyone's life easier. We have made it easy to look up classes, and see what others are saying about the class.
